About

The
Artist
Behind
The Mix

"Bridging traditional Pahadi soundscapes with the pulse of contemporary global music."

I am a professional Music Producer and Audio Engineer based in Uttarakhand, dedicated to bringing sonic clarity and creative depth to every project. With deep-rooted expertise in professional mixing, mastering, and modern music production, my workflow seamlessly bridges the gap between traditional sonic landscapes and cutting-edge audio technology.

My vision is to elevate regional Pahadi music by blending traditional acoustic elements with contemporary global sounds — Trap, EDM, Pop — creating a distinct and powerful brand that resonates with modern audiences worldwide.
